OOTY

Ooty, or Udhagamandalam, is a charming hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu. It is known for its lush green landscapes, tea gardens, and pleasant weather. Explore the botanical gardens, enjoy a ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway, and experience the serene beauty of Ooty.
